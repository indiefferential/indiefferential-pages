T-shirt with IG Handles For Each Artist On Indiefferential

By Indiefferential
May 9, 2023

What's better than supporting your favourite magazine? Supporting your favorite artists by wearing their Instagram handle on your shirt. Everyone is looking for the best promotional strategy for their brand and their new song, and the answer is right in front of our eyes, artists supporting artists. Spread the word, Indiefferential is here for every indie artist out there. A new T-Shirt will be available each month with the Instagram handles of each featured artist. Let's help indie music go further!
