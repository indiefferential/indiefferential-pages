A Time of Shadows: Gothic Horror Music Band

By Indiefferential
December 27, 2022

A Time of Shadows is a Gothic Horror Music Band Creating Orchestral and Synthesized Gothic Music with sweeping horror sounds and atmospheres.

We are true horror fans. We grew up watching horror movies and TV shows, building horror models and using SPFX makeup at our favourite time of year…Halloween. We have an extensive repertoire of musical tastes. Still, most of our influence is rock-based with a bit of Ambient music twist. We hope you enjoy listening to our music as much as we enjoy making it.
