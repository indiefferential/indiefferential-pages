Laura Pieri Releases “Daughter of Demeter (ON THE DANCEFLOOR)” – A Dark, Hypnotic Twist on Feminine Rage

Indiefferential Published on July 29, 2025

Brazilian-born pop provocateur Laura Pieri returns with a sleek and seductive reinvention of her 2024 single “Daughter of Demeter.” Titled “Daughter of Demeter (ON THE DANCEFLOOR),” this slow-burning remix leads the charge for the expanded edition of her Frankie EP, Frankie (ON THE DANCEFLOOR), arriving later this summer. The track will be joined by the equally alluring “Sea of Tragedy (ON THE DANCEFLOOR),” offering a fresh perspective on Pieri’s emotionally charged soundscape.

 



The original “Daughter of Demeter” was an electrified scream—a cathartic anthem born of heartbreak and fury. This new version trades raw defiance for calculated confidence, drawing power from restraint.

“This remix is what happens when that fire simmers into something slower, sharper, and slyer,” Pieri explains. “There’s a defiance in turning pain into pleasure. It lounges in heartbreak with style. It's rage on vacation.”

Produced with a hypnotic pulse and wrapped in cinematic textures, the remix magnifies Pieri’s fearless lyricism while expanding the emotional terrain of her artistry. Where Frankie was a declaration, Frankie (ON THE DANCEFLOOR) is a transformation.

“It made me rethink how we process and present emotion, especially feminine emotion,” says Pieri. “Sometimes healing looks like dancing. Sometimes grief shows up in a slow sway.”

Born in São Paulo and now splitting her time between New York and Los Angeles, Laura Pieri continues to blur the lines between high art and high energy, balancing the intensity of an MBA program by day with the liberation of pop stardom by night. Her music fuses glossy pop production with the deep emotional beats of her Brazilian heritage—music that dares you to feel deeply and move freely.

Frankie (ON THE DANCEFLOOR) builds on the powerful foundation laid by her original Frankie EP, released in May 2024 alongside an emotionally resonant short film. Co-written by an all-female team and executive produced by Skyler Cocco, the project marked Pieri’s defiant return from the brink of walking away—an artistic rebirth in real time.

With the arrival of Frankie (ON THE DANCEFLOOR), Pieri brings her voice back to the stage with even more clarity, sensuality, and control, ready to turn heartbreak into heat.
