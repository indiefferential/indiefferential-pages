Adik Angel: Blending Electro, Dance, and Heritage in On the Move (Remix)

Indiefferential Published on March 5, 2025

Swiss-based composer Adik Angel is back with a fresh take on her signature electro-dance sound. Her latest release, On the Move (Remix), debuted on August 10, 2024, accompanied by an electrifying music video on YouTube. Originally released in April 2024, the track fuses old-school disco vibes with hip-hop and breakdance influences, showcasing Adik’s flair for blending classic and contemporary styles.

A native of Plovdiv, Bulgaria, Adik Angel has cultivated a career that spans multiple genres, from electro-pop and dance to ambient and world music. Her compositions often incorporate Balkan and oriental rhythms, as heard in her remix of Heartbeat and the deeply personal album Ancient Roots. Her latest full-length work, EVASIONS, continues this artistic exploration, offering listeners a dynamic fusion of cultural influences and modern electronic production.



Adik’s musical journey began at an early age. Raised in a musically inclined family, she trained in classical piano and won first place in “Swiss Youth Music” at just 12 years old. Inspired by the greats—Mozart and Bach—she soon discovered her passion for composition, seamlessly transitioning into pop, rock, and electronic music by her teenage years. After studying classical and contemporary music at the Conservatory, she expanded her expertise in production, working as a Recording-Production Assistant at Geneva’s SAE Institute and interning with French producer Benoit Kaufmann at Lake Side Studio.

Her discography is as extensive as it is diverse. Over the past decade, she has released seven albums and numerous singles, including Time to Fly, New Sky, So Far Away, and the award-winning Saved Time (Remix), which garnered recognition in TJPL Magazine and the Unsigned Only Music Competition. Adik’s music has also been spotlighted in publications like LIFOTI Magazine and Bulgaria’s Maritza newspaper, and she has been interviewed on Radio Sofia.

Always innovating, Adik collaborates with a variety of artists, including Bulgarian guitarist Valentin Vasilev - Val on tracks like Mission Remix and 1001 Hopes, and Swiss film composer Rachel Nusbaumer on Stardust Reorchestrated. She continues to push creative boundaries, producing and directing her own music videos to complement her sonic landscapes.

With On the Move (Remix), Adik Angel reaffirms her ability to merge nostalgia with forward-thinking beats, solidifying her place as a visionary in the electro-dance scene. To explore her latest works and press features, visit www.adikangel.com.
