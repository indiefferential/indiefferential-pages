Video Interview

By Indiefferential
September 30, 2025

Thank you for being part of this feature! We have sent you 8 interview questions about your music and your latest release. All we need is a short video of you answering them. Send the raw video file (no need to edit) in MP4 or MOV format via Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer at indiefferential@gmail.com (RE: Video Interview)

Guidelines for Filming:

  1. Length: Please keep your full video under 5 minutes. We’ll edit it into shorter clips for TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts.

  2. Lighting: Use a ring light or record in a well-lit space (natural daylight near a window works great). Avoid backlighting so your face is clear.

  3. Background: Choose a clean, simple backdrop—a neutral wall, studio setup, or something that reflects your vibe.

  4. Framing: Record in portrait (vertical) mode on your phone, chest-up or shoulders-up, so it looks good on mobile. Tip for more engagement: You can also try to record the footage in a way similar to a podcast, not facing the camera

  1. Sound: Film in a quiet environment to avoid background noise. Speak clearly and with energy.

  2. Delivery: Be yourself! Fans love seeing your personality shine through. Feel free to smile, laugh, or gesture while talking.

  3. Format: Send the raw video file (no need to edit) in MP4 or MOV format via Google Drive, Dropbox, or WeTransfer at indiefferential@gmail.com
