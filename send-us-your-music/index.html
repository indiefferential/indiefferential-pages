Send Us Your Music

By Indiefferential
October 2, 2024

Indiefferential is always here for indie and upcoming artists! Feel free to submit your music or project and we will be more than happy to include it in our printed magazine and website. Kindly consider sending your music through the following platforms and help us continue what we love, sharing great music with our audience. We have submission tiers starting from 2$.

Indiefferential is partnered up with Musosoup where we provide a full or double page in our magazine and a spot in our 24/7 Online Radio Show. 

Musosoup streamlines the submission process, making it easy for our team to review your work and consider it for a prominent feature in our printed magazine. Click here to submit your music through Musosoup. You only pay once we have accepted your music and you’re ready to launch your campaign. You can start your campaign up to 90 days before your release date - giving you loads of time to generate great press coverage, build connections with curators, and promote your music. 

 For artists seeking constructive feedback along with the opportunity for a feature, we also work with Groover where you can send us your song for less than a coffee and we provide guarranteed feedback and a feature (along with other artists in a full page) in our magazine for less than a coffee. Click here to submit your music via Groover.

Indiefferential Plus provides the subscriber with unlimited access to all digital content available on the Indiefferential site, including Premium Content such as in-depth articles covering music promotion/production, entertainment and pop culture.

Why should you join Indiefferential+?

  • We feature your projects in a full page for 5$ per month in our magazine and every printed publication (2-3 every month)

  • A double page is 10$ per month

  • Promo at the end of every Instagram, Facebook & TikTok post of Indiefferential (1-2 every day)

  • We recommend your music on our website under the Artists section

  • Every member, no matter the tier they join, gain access for their projects to Indiefferential Spotlight. A QR code leading to this page is available on stickers that are distributed worldwide, placed in various clubs in Europe, on T-Shirts which aim to promote artists worldwide and on every magazine and publication we print.
