Under Starling Released Captivating New Single "Selkie"

Indiefferential Published on September 1, 2024

Ireland’s alternative rock sensation, Under Starling, is set to enchant listeners with their upcoming single "Selkie," releasing on July 26th. This latest track, rich in indie and folk rock influences, offers a poignant exploration of long-distance relationships, seamlessly weaving together myth and reality through the timeless Selkie legend, symbolizing hope and the power of connection.

"Selkie" is the second single from Under Starling’s much-anticipated debut album, “Murmurations,” which is slated for release on October 25th. The song tells the tale of a protagonist who, upon receiving a seal skin from a Selkie, embarks on a mystical, oceanic journey to reunite with their beloved. With lyrical depth, the track touches on themes of love transcending distance, resilience in the face of challenges, and personal growth, all set against the majestic and often tumultuous backdrop of the sea.

The Selkie myth has long been a part of Irish folklore, capturing the imaginations of sailors and coastal dwellers with tales of seal-people who come ashore and shed their skins to take on human form. The enduring appeal of these legends is tied to Ireland’s rich marine life, particularly the native seals that are vital to the country’s ecosystem. Under Starling is using the release of "Selkie" to draw attention to this important species, partnering with Seal Rescue Ireland. All profits from the song sales and collaborative merchandise will go directly to this charity, helping to protect and preserve Ireland’s seal population and, with it, the myth of the Selkie.

Under Starling is an alternative rock band from Ireland known for their evolving, distinctive sound. Their music, characterized by atmospheric effects like reverb, delay, and chorus, blends catchy hooks with massive soundscapes, drawing influence from iconic bands such as The Cranberries, Kings of Leon, and U2. This unique fusion creates a sound that is both euphoric and gritty, offering listeners an immersive experience.


The band kicked off 2024 with their single "Changeling," which garnered praise from prominent outlets including Hot Press, Clout, Mystic Sons, and Earmilk. Known for their compelling live performances, Under Starling provides an escape for their audiences, uniting them in a shared, euphoric experience—much like a flock of starlings in flight. With the release of their debut album on the horizon, Under Starling is poised to make a lasting impact on the music scene, pushing the boundaries of alternative rock and heralding a new era of Irish music.

Selkies, also known as seal people or sea people, are mythical beings found in Irish folklore. These creatures are said to be seals in the water, but upon coming ashore, they shed their skins to become human. The legend of the Selkie has endured over the centuries, particularly in remote coastal areas, where tales abound of humans falling in love with these enchanting beings. According to folklore, if someone finds a Selkie’s hidden skin, the Selkie cannot return to the sea, often resulting in a marriage between the human and the Selkie. The legend speaks to themes of longing, transformation, and the unbreakable ties between land and sea.
