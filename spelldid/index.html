SPELLDID - Software for Journalists created for Indiefferential

By Indiefferential
September 29, 2025

 

 

  • Created by Indiefferential for Indie Voices
    Spelldid was developed exclusively for Indiefferential, built to reflect the platform’s mission of supporting new voices in journalism and cultural commentary.

  • A Smarter Editing Tool Without AI Dependence
    Unlike most writing aids that rely on generative AI, Spelldid was crafted over six months by a team of brilliant European computer engineers using traditional programming, ensuring transparency, consistency, and control.

  • Tailored for Pop Culture Storytelling
    Spelldid goes beyond grammar. It understands music, entertainment, and internet culture, helping writers make references that land and narratives that resonate.

  • Train as You Write
    Designed with early-career journalists in mind, Spelldid not only corrects mistakes, it explains them. Think of it as a built-in writing coach, offering clarity and guidance in real-time.

  • Industry-Savvy and Up-to-Date
    Whether it’s the latest album drop, meme trend, or cultural shift, Spelldid keeps editors in the loop, ensuring their content stays timely and relevant.

  • Editorial Quality, Indie Spirit
    Built with Indiefferential’s commitment to independent journalism at its core, Spelldid blends professional-grade editing features with the creativity and freedom of indie publishing.

  • Perfect for Internal Training and Growth
    As part of Indiefferential’s editorial toolkit, Spelldid supports new contributors by streamlining onboarding and elevating writing quality across the board.
