Austin Lowman Paints the Night in “Velvet”

With “Velvet,” Austin Lowman invites listeners into a shadowy dreamscape where introspection meets slow-burning rock. This haunting ballad drapes itself in mellow guitar riffs and emotive vocals, crafting an atmosphere both tender and chilling.

The track unfolds like a midnight confession—low-lit, honest, and soul-stirring. Perfect for solitary moments, “Velvet” lingers long after its final note, echoing like a distant memory.

 

Andreas Klamm’s “Love Will Lead Thy Way” Offers a Global Message of Hope and Connection

With a voice rooted in compassion and a career spanning over 30 years of music and advocacy, Andreas Klamm returns with a stirring new single, “Love Will Lead Thy Way.” This poignant love anthem, released internationally on all major streaming platforms, echoes a timeless message: that love is the compass guiding us through life’s storms.

A renowned German-French singer-songwriter, journalist, and humanitarian, Klamm once again blends soulful lyrics with heartfelt melodies, delivering a track that speaks to the emotional core of listeners from every corner of the globe. Known for his poetic storytelling and deep emotional resonance, Klamm describes the song as “something that offers comfort, encouragement, and a sense of connection in times when the world needs it most.”

“Love Will Lead Thy Way” is not just a song—it’s a gentle call to unity, a reminder that even in the face of division and uncertainty, love remains our most powerful force. With lyrics that uplift and a melody that lingers, the track captures Klamm’s signature sincerity and global perspective, shaped by decades of work not only as a musician but also as a vocal advocate for human rights and peace.

New Laconia – The Fates: A Cosmic Dance of Destiny

ABOUT MONSTERS Unleash the Haunting Intensity of "Suffocate"

German metal/post-hardcore outfit ABOUT MONSTERS return with Suffocate, their latest single under Redfield Records. A brooding and relentless track, Suffocate delves into the harrowing grip of toxic relationships—where manipulation, emotional detachment, and suffocating pressure slowly erode one’s sense of self.

Crafted by guitarist Florian Brunemann and vocalist Madeleine ‘Maddy’ Wiebe, with additional contributions from Julian Breucker (Smash Into Pieces, Future Palace, Annisokay, Beyond The Black), the single thrives on a fusion of crushing riffs, searing vocals, and an unshakable chorus. With Breucker spearheading production and Christoph Wieczorek (Annisokay, Electric Callboy, Future Palace) handling mixing and mastering, Suffocate delivers an unfiltered emotional gut punch, capturing the weight of despair and the fight for self-preservation.

Recorded in 2024 at Sawdust Recordings in Halle (Saale), Germany, Suffocate is the latest step in ABOUT MONSTERS’ sonic evolution. The band—comprising Wiebe (vocals), Brunemann (guitar), Sönke Heuer (guitar), Torben Heuer (bass), and Alexander Tepe (drums)—has solidified their reputation for blending raw aggression with haunting melody.

December Top Releases

Rita Mercury Unveils "Hurricane (Reimagined)" – A Storm of Passion and Resilience

Following her powerful debut with “Nobody,” Rita Mercury returns with her captivating new single, “Hurricane (Reimagined).” This evocative ballad, layered with a light rock edge, pairs Mercury’s exquisite vocals with a rich instrumental palette of soft guitar, delicate piano, and resonant bass lines.


“Hurricane (Reimagined)” unfolds the tale of a love as tumultuous as it is passionate. “Our love is a hurricane, turbulent and wild. Don’t let go of my hand, don’t leave me behind,” Mercury sings, drawing listeners into a heartfelt journey of resilience and hope. The song’s lush metaphors and vivid imagery explore the fragility and beauty of human connection, creating an emotional resonance that lingers long after the final note.

With its blend of sensuality, depth, and emotional honesty, “Hurricane (Reimagined)” continues to solidify Rita Mercury’s place as an artist who fearlessly wears her heart on her sleeve. Her music inspires listeners to embrace the storms of love and find strength amid the chaos.

December Top Releases

 

Louise Goffin: Brooklyn-Born, Laurel Canyon-Souled

Brooklyn-born multi-instrumentalist and singer-songwriter Louise Goffin has always been deeply curious about music’s power to connect and transform. With creativity coursing through her veins, thanks to her renowned songwriter parents Carole King and Gerry Goffin, Louise grew up in an environment that was as inspiring as it was complex—her father’s bipolar disorder adding layers of depth and introspection to her artistry.

Now in what she describes as the most fulfilling phase of her career, Louise’s music continues to blossom in unexpected ways. Drawing inspiration from the rich legacy of Laurel Canyon, her lyrics are deeply personal and introspective, reminiscent of the storytelling giants she grew up admiring.

 

Stephen Dowd, a talented Canadian songwriter based in Ottawa, Ontario, has been steadily carving his path in the music scene with his heartfelt lyrics and eclectic musical style. Over the course of 2024, he has recorded and released three captivating tracks, each showcasing a unique blend of genres and his evolving artistic vision.

The journey began in May with the release of “You’re Beautiful To Me,” a tender ballad that resonated deeply with listeners for its sincere lyrics and emotive delivery. Dowd followed up in August with the energetic Pop/Rock anthem “Heaven Won’t Wait,” a collaborative effort co-written with Gareth Auden-Hole. This track added a dynamic edge to his repertoire, blending infectious rhythms with introspective storytelling.

Now, Dowd has taken a bold step into the Dance/Pop realm with his latest single, “(Ça S’en Vient) It’s Coming Can You Feel It.” This third release marks a significant departure from his earlier work, showcasing his versatility and ability to craft songs that pulse with energy and excitement.

 

Fuentes De Vida, a bilingual singer-songwriter from Brooklyn, New York, crafts a sonic landscape where raw alternative rock meets introspective, socially charged storytelling. Drawing inspiration from icons like Patti Smith, Björk, Imagine Dragons, and Nirvana, the music intertwines bold energy with themes of addiction, grief, love, and human connection. Each track is designed not just to be heard but to resonate deeply, sparking reflection and connection.

TheJuZShoW Debuts Within the Vision: A Bold Evolution in Alternative EDM

Crafted entirely by TheJuZShoW herself, the 12-track project showcases her skills as a writer, producer, mixer, and master of her craft. With vocals recorded in her home studio, Within the Vision isn’t just an album—it’s a sonic manifesto.


“This album is like nothing I’ve ever written before,” she explains. “It’s the sound I’ve always been chasing—a step in the right direction, both creatively and personally.”

Drawing influence from industrial, electronic, and alternative titans like Jean-Michel Jarre and KMFDM, the record delivers a rich, immersive listening experience. Each track pulses with a unique energy, blending haunting melodies, thunderous beats, and introspective lyrics that redefine the boundaries of modern EDM.

With Within the Vision, TheJuZShoW invites listeners into her world—a place where innovation reigns, and every track tells a story worth hearing. Don’t miss the chance to experience her bold debut for yourself.

 

Social Gravy Returns with Electrifying New Single, “Lone Wolf”

Social Gravy, helmed by Brad Kohn and Vee Bordukov, roars back onto the scene with their first release since 2020: “Lone Wolf.” This high-energy, riff-driven track is just the beginning of a new chapter for the band, as they gear up to release a series of singles culminating in a highly anticipated EP.

Clocking in at just over two minutes, “Lone Wolf” is a sonic firecracker. Rooted in the swagger of Aerosmith, the grit of Soundgarden, and the bluesy allure of The Black Keys, the track hooks listeners with its infectious core guitar riff and thundering rhythm. Brad Kohn’s commanding vocal delivery adds depth, pouring emotion into lyrics that explore themes of longing and escape. Lines like, “It’s a long way just to love you, short shot to the moon,” highlight Social Gravy’s knack for blending raw intensity with heartfelt melodies.

Known for their riff-heavy yet deeply melodic sound, Social Gravy has carved out a niche as “romantic rock’n’rollers” who balance high-energy tracks with emotional authenticity. Their past work, including the acclaimed 2017 album Behind Every Yes and the 2020 EP A Different Kind, earned praise from outlets like The Big Takeover and Spill Magazine, with the latter describing their music as “quality rocking pop.”

“Lone Wolf” continues this legacy, spotlighting the band’s ability to craft memorable, emotionally resonant tracks without compromising their indie rock roots. With a sound that’s both nostalgic and refreshingly modern, Social Gravy remains dedicated to keeping the spirit of rock alive.

Available on all major streaming platforms starting November 22, 2024, “Lone Wolf” promises to set the stage for an exciting new era of music from Social Gravy.

Dasvibes and Wiski D Light Up the EDM Scene with Explosive New Single Bring Dat Come

International music innovators Dasvibes (DJ Wade) and Wiski D are making waves with the release of their electrifying EDM dance track, Bring Dat Come. Dropping on all major streaming platforms on November 22, 2024, this high-energy anthem fuses EDM’s pulse-pounding beats with the vibrant rhythms of Caribbean dancehall. The track, mastered by legendary engineer Leon Zervos at the iconic Studios 301 in Sydney, promises a polished and powerful sound that’s destined to ignite dance floors worldwide.

Bring Dat Come stands as a bold departure for Dasvibes and Wiski D, pushing the boundaries of their artistic style. While their previous hit, Bring That Come, leaned heavily into dancehall, this track ventures into the exhilarating world of EDM, showcasing Dasvibes' innovative production techniques and Wiski D’s commanding lyrical delivery. Together, they create a seamless blend of global sounds that captures the essence of both genres.


With Leon Zervos’ expert mastering—a name behind hits from Maroon 5, Muse, and Aerosmith—the track is elevated to new heights. Zervos' touch ensures a crystal-clear and explosive sound that’s ready for clubs, festivals, and playlists alike.

Dasvibes, an Australian-Jamaican DJ and producer based in Brisbane, is celebrated for blending his Jamaican roots with cutting-edge electronic music. Renowned for his inventive sampling and energetic live sets, Dasvibes continues to redefine EDM with his genre-defying approach.

Wiski D, hailing from Kingston, Jamaica, is a rising star in the dancehall scene. Influenced by the infectious rhythms of 90s dancehall and his Christian upbringing, he has carved out a unique space in the genre. His innovative style and magnetic stage presence have positioned him as a global voice for contemporary dancehall.

Actor-Turned-Singer-Songwriter David Raine Steps Into the Spotlight with Soulful New Singles

David Raine has spent years captivating audiences on screen, but the seasoned TV and film actor is now channeling his storytelling talents into music. With a history as a singer-songwriter that predates his acting career, Raine’s passion for music has long been a deeply personal endeavor. Over the years, he’s contributed songs to soundtracks and written for other artists, but now, he’s stepping into the spotlight with a series of solo releases that showcase his introspective, thought-provoking lyricism.

Raine describes his songwriting style as contemplative, often weaving clever imagery into lyrics that reflect how he communicates in real life—authentic and meaningful. His recent releases, PRAY and ORION, are already making waves, offering a glimpse into his knack for pairing poetic depth with relatable emotion.

Next up is SILENT, a hauntingly beautiful piano and guitar ballad slated for release on December 6, 2024. This track promises to be a standout moment in his growing discography, blending stripped-down instrumentation with heartfelt vulnerability.

David’s plan is simple yet ambitious: to build a strong EP’s worth of music that resonates with listeners and sparks meaningful connections. By releasing singles every few months, he hopes to gradually gain feedback, identify his audience, and refine his approach to self-promotion. The ultimate goal? To book live shows, from intimate gatherings to larger tours, and cultivate a loyal fan base.

“I want to know who my listeners are and what connects with them,” Raine says. “Music has always been personal to me, but now I want to share it and see where it takes me.”

Five Headed Cobra: Delivering an Alt-Rock Punch with “Screaming From The Mountain” 

Five Headed Cobra continues to make waves with their distinct, high-energy sound, blending raw power with dynamic intricacy. The band’s latest offerings, Screaming From The Mountain and Beast Brain, deliver a captivating experience for lovers of the genre, showcasing their ability to craft tracks that are both intense and innovative.

This track stands out for its explosive energy and intricate details, making each listen a discovery of its own. Built on scratchy guitars and driving rhythms, Screaming From The Mountain offers a faster-paced sound punctuated with power-packed nuances. The result is a dynamic, adrenaline-fueled track that captivates from start to finish, pulling listeners into its electrifying atmosphere.

For fans of alternative rock, this song is a straightforward yet richly layered offering. Its immediate appeal gives way to deeper intrigue, culminating in a finale that feels both natural and thrilling.
