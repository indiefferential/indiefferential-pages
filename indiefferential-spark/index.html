A magazine created by artists featuring new music, games, books and art projects worldwide. The physical copies of the magazine are printed in the U.S. although we are working with editors both in the U.S. and Europe. A T-Shirt featuring names and IG handles of every featured artist so artists can help each other grow their audience.

Running a printed magazine in 2025 isn’t easy. We've recently faced another 5% increase in printing costs, that’s why every donation truly makes a difference.Your support helps us keep print alive, and as a thank-you, you’ll receive monthly promotion in both our printed magazine and on our website.

You can buy us a coffee (5$) and you can have a full page every month just for your projects on every printed issue of What's New? (3-4 per month). Perks:

  1. A full page in our magazine a QR code leading back to your release/social media.
  2. 2 additional pages only in the digital version of the magazine since there are many readers interested in the online version.
  3.  
  4. You music/project on our website (Spotlight & Artists section)
  5. Online Banners promoting your project between our articles with links (instead of Google ads)
  6. We add the full page at the end of every post on our social media (carousel) 
  7. One additional photo on every carousel post on Instagram (we post 4-7 days per week)
  8. Your music on Indiefferential radio streaming music 24/7 online
  9. We suggest your music/project to our readers via our mailing list (1-2 mails per month)
  10. Billboard promo support (we run weekly promo campaigns on billboards across the world)
