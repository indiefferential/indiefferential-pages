Guest Feature Guide: Join Us on the Air

By Indiefferential
June 25, 2026

Hey there,

We are thrilled to feature you on our upcoming programming! Inspired by the classic, intimate style of late-night curation shows, we’re building a unique audio experience.

Instead of a standard, rigid live interview, we want to feature your raw, authentic voice storytelling alongside your music. Your clips will be featured on Indiefferential Radio, chopped into bite-sized pieces for our TikTok & Instagram, and highlighted on a brand-new Artist Platform launching later this month.

Here is how to get involved in 3 easy steps.

The Setup (Keep it Simple)

You don't need a fancy recording studio for this, a smartphone works perfectly.

  • Where: Find a quiet room with minimal echo.

  • Technique: Hold the phone a few inches from your mouth, slightly to the side (so you don't blow into the mic). Just speak naturally, like you're talking to a friend.

The Prompts (Pick Your Flavor)

Please record a separate audio file for each response. Start by introducing yourself: "Hey, this is [Name/Band] and you're listening to..."

Then, choose 2 or 3 of the prompts below to answer:

  1. What is the weirdest or most unexpected inspiration behind your latest track?
  2. Tell us a quick, funny, or bizarre story from a recent live show or studio session.
  3. What is a piece of cheap gear, a thrift-store instrument, or a random tool you use that punches way above its weight class?
  4. Tell us about a mistake you made while recording that ended up staying in the final track because it sounded too cool to cut.
  5. What advice would you give to upcoming artists out there?
  6. What do you think of AI in music?

Once you're done, please send us your files at: indiefferential@gmail.com

Please include:

  • The audio files (.wav or .mp3 preferred)

  • Your primary Instagram/TikTok handles (so we can tag you in the video snippets!)

  • A high-res press photo or cover art

🌟 Why This Rules For You

By sending us these quick clips, you’ll be featured across:

  1. Our Weekly Radio Show: Immersed into a curated playlist.

  2. Social Snippets: Highly engaging, stylized video reels on TikTok/IG pushing traffic to your music.

  3. The New Platform: Early-access exposure on our upcoming artist hub launching this month.

Thank you for creating with us. If you have any questions, just hit reply!
