Contact

If you are interested in a feature on Indiefferential, feel free to tell us more about your music, product or project at: indiefferential@gmail.com or simply send us a direct message.

You can also submit music to Indiefferential according to your needs.

Submit your music effortlessly through Musosoup, our preferred platform for discovering and featuring new artists. Musosoup streamlines the submission process, making it easy for our team to review your work and consider it for a prominent feature in our printed magazine. Click here to submit your music through Musosoup. (Full page & Double Page Printed Features Available)

Submission for Guaranteed Feedback: For artists seeking constructive feedback along with the opportunity for a feature, we encourage you to submit your music through Groover. This platform ensures that your work is not only considered for our magazine feature but also guarantees valuable feedback from industry professionals. Click here to submit your music via Groover. (A feature in our printed magazine with other artists on the same page)

We also have a support page where you can buy us a coffee (5$) and you can have a full page every month just for your projects on every printed issue of What's New? (3-4 per month).
