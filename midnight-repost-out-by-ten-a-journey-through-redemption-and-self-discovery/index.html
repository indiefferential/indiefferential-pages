Midnight Repost Debut EP ‘Out By Ten’ – A Journey Through Redemption and Self-Discovery

By Indiefferential
October 2, 2024

Midnight Repost has released their debut EP Out By Ten, a powerful exploration of redemption, forgiveness, and self-acceptance. Spanning five captivating tracks, the record showcases the band’s creative evolution, blending pop, rock, indie, and funk influences to craft a sonic experience that draws listeners into their rich, diverse soundscape.


Recorded in a remote studio in Norway, the EP captures the raw magic of Midnight Repost’s transformative journey. At the core of the project is a character arc written by frontman Liam Priestnall, whose deeply personal lyrics trace a troubled, selfish, and arrogant figure’s metamorphosis into peace and self-acceptance. Through slick synths, driving drums, and luscious guitar licks, the EP weaves a narrative of introspection and growth.

Having just wrapped up their UK summer tour, Midnight Repost has rapidly gained recognition for their fresh and innovative approach to classic genres. Originally formed in 2021 as Liam's solo project, the group has since expanded into a trio and achieved success both live and digitally. With support from BBC Radio 1, BBC Essex, and BBC Rapal, the band’s energetic performances and infectious tunes have been making waves across the industry.

The EP’s highly anticipated release follows a string of singles like Stay, Blue Eyes, and Fantasy, which have already left fans eager for more. Out By Ten, set to drop on September 6th, promises to solidify Midnight Repost’s place in the spotlight with its irresistible blend of intricate rhythms, relatable lyrics, and moody melodies. Fans can look forward to a live release party this autumn to celebrate the EP.
