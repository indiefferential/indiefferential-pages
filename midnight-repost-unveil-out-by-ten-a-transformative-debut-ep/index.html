Midnight Repost Unveil "Out By Ten": A Transformative Debut EP

Indiefferential Published on November 13, 2024

Essex-based band Midnight Repost comes at the ready with their debut EP, Out By Ten. Explored through this five-track EP are ideas of redemption, forgiveness, and self-acceptance. This first collection shows how their sound is changing: a mix of pop, rock, indie, and funk. They recorded Out By Ten in a quiet studio in Norway, which helps show both Midnight Repost's creativity and their desire to explore sound.


Each of the tracks tells part of the story of the EP. The lyrics, which are by the frontman Liam Priestnall, go through the outline of a troubled character who battles with selfishness and arrogance. Boasted with lush, expansive guitars, smooth synths, and strong beats, a very impactful journey transpires—from selfishness to insight into one's self, a clear image of personal change.

 

 



Midnight Repost has built up a strong fanbase over the summer, gigging up and down the UK. They added their own creative style to those classic styles of music, which has gotten them played on national radio, including BBC Radio 1, BBC Essex, and BBC Rapal. Their songs "Stay," "Blue Eyes," and "Fantasy" from the EP have created excitement. Their official EP release is Out By Ten on September 6 and will surely bring the band even more attention.


Out By Ten, Midnight Repost has found the right balance between strong feelings and smooth music. The best part about this EP has to be the guitar playing—that is spot-on and heartfelt. The guitar parts within each song are well incorporated, creating an interesting blend of dark, powerful riffs with soft and airy sounds. All of these elements create a three-dimensional feel, making the space bigger and allowing a deep level of involvement in the story for listeners.



Mixing on this EP is truly great; it's so clear, allowing every single part to shine while making the emotional changes in the EP stronger. From synths to vocals, every part has a space of its own, but they all fit very well within the overall sound. The result is that the listening experience is full where every part, from the strong beat of the drums to the softest sounds of the bass, has been crafted and mixed with an artist's skill. It just feels lively, giving Out By Ten both a sense of closeness and depth that invites listeners to find new details each time they put it on.

 

Join Midnight Re-Post this fall for a unique live release party. Get ready for a fun experience with complex rhythms, memorable lyrics, and infectious melodies.
