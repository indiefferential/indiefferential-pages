Cassie Fireman has dropped a music video for latest single "Feel Like Gold"

By Indiefferential
May 6, 2023

'Feels Like Gold' is basically a love letter to my younger, more reckless self. It speaks to that little child inside that never felt good enough or like she was always messing things up somehow. It shines the light on those parts of ourselves we've felt ashamed of and invites them back to the party. Like saying "Hey, you made it! You're imperfect, we get it and you no longer have to hide". I also just hope this song makes you feel good whether you're dancing to it, blasting it with windows down in the car or getting ready for a night on the town...I hope you feel and know you are gold when you hear it. - Cassie Fireman
