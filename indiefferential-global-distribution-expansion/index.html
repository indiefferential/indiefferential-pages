Indiefferential Global Distribution Expansion

By Indiefferential
July 20, 2026

We are excited to announce that Indiefferential Magazine has expanded its international distribution, making our print and digital editions available to a worldwide network of booksellers, online retailers, libraries, and educational institutions.

As our latest issue is catalogued across global distribution channels, readers may begin to find Indiefferential Magazine through participating retailers and library platforms in their region.

North America
Amazon • Barnes & Noble • Walmart • Books-A-Million • Bookshop.org • Powell's Books • Indigo • Chapters • Hudson Booksellers • University Bookstores • Independent Bookstores

United Kingdom & Europe
Waterstones • Foyles • Blackwell's • Gardners • Adlibris • Bokus • Libri • Casa del Libro • FNAC • El Corte Inglés • Cultura • Empik • Saxo • Bol • Libris

Australia & New Zealand
Booktopia • Dymocks • The Nile • Fishpond • Wheelers • James Bennett • ALS Library Services

Asia & Middle East
Books Kinokuniya • Kinokuniya Singapore • Jarir Bookstore • Magrudy's • Kyobo Book Centre • Yes24 • Aladin • Sapna Book House

Libraries & Academic Institutions
OverDrive • Hoopla • Bibliotheca • EBSCO • ProQuest • Follett • Mackin • Odilo • Wheelers ePlatform • BibliU

Digital Reading Platforms
Amazon Kindle • Apple Books • Kobo • Google Play Books • Barnes & Noble Nook • Everand • ebooks.com • Gardners Digital • De Marque

Please note that availability may vary by country, retailer, platform, and catalog update schedules. Some retailers may list the magazine for order immediately, while others may add it over time or upon customer request.

Thank you for supporting independent music journalism.
