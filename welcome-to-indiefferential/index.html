Welcome to Indiefferential

A magazine created by musicians and producers to promote new releases, unsigned musicians, and art projects worldwide. We plan to make this website a place for artists to share their work and grow their audience. The magazine will be available for free and will feature most of the content found on the website. In addition to music and artists, Indiefferntial covers live shows, festivals and all the events where great art is found. In all cases, the magazine seeks to avoid the “same old” stories seeking out fresh material.
