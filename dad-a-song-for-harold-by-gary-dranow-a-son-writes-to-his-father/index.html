'Dad, A Song for Harold' by Gary Dranow: A Son Writes To His Father

By Indiefferential
February 29, 2024

"Discover the heartfelt fusion of rock and storytelling in Gary Dranow's latest single, 'Dad, A Song for Harold,' released in collaboration with The Manic Emotions. This emotive track, inspired by a touching Christmas letter from Matthew DeBlanc to his father Harold, captures the essence of paternal love and wisdom.

With a lineup boasting talents from across the globe, including Australia and Ukraine, the band's diverse influences and personal connections shine through. Meticulously produced and rich with raw emotion, this song not only pays homage to the bond between father and son but also showcases the transformative power of music. Dive into the melodic tale of fatherly love and follow Gary Dranow and The Manic Emotions on their musical journey."

The band's formation transcends geographical boundaries, with members spanning Park City, Utah, Springfield, Utah, Melbourne, Australia, and various cities in Ukraine. Drawing inspiration from their personal experiences and diverse influences, their music pays homage to the deep friendship between Gary Dranow and Harold Deblanc.

Gary Dranow and The Manic Emotions have garnered acclaim for their online performances during Tuesday night Livestreams. They've also received recognition in various music magazines and blogs, thanks to their unwavering dedication to their craft and their genuine connection with their audience.

"Music is more than just notes and lyrics; it's a medium to express the depth of human emotions and experiences. 'Dad, A Song for Harold' is our tribute to the enduring power of fatherly love and the profound impact it has on shaping our lives." - Gary Dranow

The band's journey is marked by overcoming personal challenges and channeling their emotions into their music. Their collaboration on "Dad, A Song For Harold" reflects a poignant moment in Gary and Harold's lives. "Dad, A Song for Harold" and other tracks from the band undergo meticulous production, with each member contributing their unique skills.

The result is a sound that seamlessly blends raw emotion with contemporary sensibilities. Their music explores a range of themes, including friendship, loss, and resilience, drawing from their personal experiences and musical journey. "Dad, A Song For Harold" stands as a testament to the power of love and the healing potential of music.
