Head Sound - Optimistic A Dream Pop Journey

By Indiefferential
March 15, 2024

Who are the band members and what do they play?

Stewbie plays rhythm guitar and vocals, Richard plays lead guitar and vocals, Aiden plays bass, Phil plays drums.

What are the band's main influences?

My Bloody Valentine, Slowdive, Radiohead, and Brian Eno.

Have you played any notable gigs, festivals or other events? Likewise radio or TV appearances?

Our music has been played on local radio WEXT and EQX.

Are there any other interesting or amusing happenings – no matter how small – that the band have been involved with that would be of interest to those who like your music?

We made two bands to open and close for Head Sound with the same members. One is Americana and the other is Experimental. We dress up in cowboy hats and bolo ties, and astronaut helmets respectively.
