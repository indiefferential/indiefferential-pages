HalfcutLemon released “Disturbance” in front of their next album.

Indiefferential Published on September 7, 2025

Ahead of their much awaited new album coming later this month, Copenhagen-based alternative rock group HalfcutLemon will release their third song, "Disturbance" on September 5th. Famous for combining unadulterated punk passion with movie depth, the band once again offers a song that is both provocative and amusingly fantastical.
Reflecting on technology, social pressures, and even the daily warmth of a radiator, Disturbance creates layered instrumentation with enigmatic lyrics. Under its lighthearted veneer, the song investigates ideas of conspiracy culture and our readiness to be guided and comforted.


The single includes choral textures from Persille Ingerslev, atmospheric synths from Peter Peter, and extra vocals by Flemming. The song urges listeners to stop, consider, and grin at the ridiculousness of life with bursts of broken imagery and rhythmic mantras.
