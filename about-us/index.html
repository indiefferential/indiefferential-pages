About

“In all cases, the magazine seeks to avoid the same old stories seeking out fresh material.”

A magazine created by artists featuring new music, games, books and art projects worldwide. The physical copies of the magazine are printed in the U.S. although we are working with editors both in the U.S. and Europe. The online magazine is available for free and features most of the content found on the website. We interview artists monthly and we introduce them to our readers via quick interviews.

Welcome to Indiefferential Magazine, your go-to source for the latest in music and art. We are a dynamic publication that takes pride in our monthly interviews with talented artists, ensuring our readers are always up-to-date with the freshest talent and most captivating stories. With a focus on both printed and digital content, we strive to showcase music and art projects from around the globe.

You can read what fellow artists have to say about our magazine and their opinion on our work on featuring their work and projects.

Our dedicated team of passionate writers and interviewers has engaged with over 1,000 musicians, delving deep into their experiences, inspirations, and unique journeys. At Indiefferential Magazine, we believe that asking similar questions to each artist allows us to uncover universal truths about the creative process. By posing consistent inquiries, we aim to create a rich tapestry of insights that can inspire and inform both aspiring artists and devoted fans. Through these interviews, we provide a window into the artist's world, showcasing their struggles, triumphs, and the unique aspects of their creative process.

In addition to our main publication, we also take pride in our "What's New?" printed publication. This platform allows us to share the latest music releases, art exhibitions, and creative projects in real-time. With a finger on the pulse of the ever-evolving creative landscape, we provide a curated space where artists can shine, and readers can discover the next big thing.

Our global reach is one of our greatest strengths. At Indiefferential Magazine, we understand that creativity knows no boundaries. Whether it's an underground indie band in New York, a street artist in Tokyo, or a sculptor in Paris, we seek out talent from every corner of the world. We are committed to bridging the gap between cultures, unearthing hidden gems, and fostering a sense of unity through the universal language of music and art.

Come explore with us as we continue to champion the voices of talented musicians and artists, and embark on an adventure that transcends boundaries, defies conventions, and leaves an indelible mark on your soul. Welcome to Indiefferential Magazine – where music and art collide in harmony.
