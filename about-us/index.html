About

“In all cases, the magazine seeks to avoid the same old stories seeking out fresh material.”

A magazine created by artists featuring new music, games, books, and art projects worldwide. The physical copies of the magazine are printed in the U.S., although we are working with editors both in the U.S. and Europe. The online magazine is available for free and features most of the content found on the website. We interview artists monthly and introduce them to our readers via quick interviews.

You can read what fellow artists have to say about our magazine and their opinions on our work featuring their projects.

Our global reach is one of our greatest strengths. At Indiefferential Magazine, we understand that creativity knows no boundaries. Whether it's an underground indie band in New York, a street artist in Tokyo, or a sculptor in Paris, we seek out talent from every corner of the world. We are committed to bridging the gap between cultures, unearthing hidden gems, and fostering a sense of unity through the universal language of music and art. Come explore with us as we continue to champion the voices of talented musicians and artists, and embark on an adventure that transcends boundaries, defies conventions, and leaves an indelible mark on your soul.
