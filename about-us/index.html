 About Indiefferential

Independent music deserves more than an algorithm. It deserves a lasting story.

Since 2012, Indiefferential has been discovering, reviewing, and championing independent artists from around the world. What began as an online publication has grown into a respected platform that connects emerging musicians with dedicated music fans, industry professionals, and fellow artists.

In 2021, we expanded beyond the screen and launched Indiefferential Magazine in print, because great music deserves a place you can hold in your hands. Unlike a social media post that disappears in hours, a printed magazine becomes part of collections, coffee tables, studios, record stores, and libraries, giving artists a lasting feature that can be revisited for years.

Over the years, some of the most influential independent artists and music professionals have worked with Indiefferential to share their stories, promote new releases, and reach new audiences. We believe every artist has a story worth telling, whether they're releasing their first single or headlining international festivals.

Our reputation has earned recognition throughout the music industry. Indiefferential was featured in Oren Sharon's list of the Best Music Blogs to Get Your Music Featured in 2025, recognizing our commitment to thoughtful editorial coverage and artist discovery.

Quality journalism is at the heart of everything we publish. Our editorial team is made up of professional musicians and experienced writers, each holding at least a master's degree, combining academic expertise with real-world industry experience to deliver insightful interviews, reviews, and features.

Why Print Still Matters

In a world of endless scrolling, print offers something different:

  • Longevity: A magazine doesn't disappear in a crowded feed—it stays on shelves, desks, and in personal collections.

  • Credibility: Being featured in a professionally produced publication adds a tangible achievement to an artist's press portfolio.

  • Shareability: Print issues are passed between fans, venues, studios, labels, and industry professionals, extending the life of every feature.

  • Global Reach: Every issue helps independent artists connect with readers who actively seek out new music instead of relying solely on algorithms.

At Indiefferential, we're committed to giving independent artists the professional coverage they deserve, both online and in print, and to creating a publication that celebrates creativity, originality, and the future of independent music.

Because independent music isn't a trend. It's the future.
