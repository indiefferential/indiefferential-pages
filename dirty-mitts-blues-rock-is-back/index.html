Dirty Mitts: Blues Rock Is Back!

Indiefferential Published on June 11, 2024

Formed in 2022, Dirty Mitts has quickly become a force to be reckoned with in the rock scene, blending bluesy nuances with retro vibes and contagious vigor. Their EP ‘Hands Off’ has garnered widespread acclaim, with tracks like "Ride the Storm," "Women," and "You Better Run! (Villains Theme)" captivating audiences worldwide.

‘Electric Kid’ sees Dirty Mitts diving deeper into their musical evolution, delivering a track that is both raw and introspective. Ghazi's lyrics paint a vivid picture of his journey from a shy kid to a fearless individual, navigating the complexities of life with resilience and determination.

The vocal range of Tommy, the tight playing of the rest of the band and that production makes you wonder if we discovered time travel. This is what an old school Rock should sound like! 

Lead vocalist Tommy, bassist Matt, guitarist Mo, and drummer Mateusz bring their signature blend of high-octane energy and thunderous percussion to ‘Electric Kid’ creating an anthem that is as powerful as it is evocative.

With its infectious riffs and gritty vocals, the song is sure to resonate with fans old and new. "Electric Kid" is now available on all major streaming platforms.
