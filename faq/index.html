Q: What is your billboard promo plan?

We primarily use static image billboards, which allows us to keep the promotions budget-friendly. These are often featured at key spots such as bus stops and EV charging stations, providing high visibility at an affordable cost. We also include a QR code (for digital billboards) that passersby can scan to instantly access our latest edition or specific features. We run those ads during the month and they usually lead to our magazine page so more people can read and order our magazine.

Q: Can artists receive complimentary issues of the magazine?

A: Unfortunately, at this time, we are not able to send complimentary issues to the artists featured in Indiefferential Magazine. However, we greatly appreciate their contributions and strive to provide them with a platform to showcase their work to a wider audience.

Here are some key reasons why we can't send complimentary printed issues just yet:

Resource Constraints: At the moment, our resources, both in terms of finances and manpower, are allocated to maintaining the quality of content, production, and distribution of our digital and print editions. Expanding our operations to include complimentary printed issues necessitates additional resources, which we are actively working to secure.

Logistical Complexities: Coordinating the production and distribution of complimentary printed issues involves intricate logistics. We want to ensure a seamless and efficient process that guarantees timely delivery to all artists. Currently, we are refining our logistical framework to accommodate this expansion.

Despite these challenges, we want to emphasize our dedication to making complimentary printed issues a reality for artists in the future.
