Cloud Ray feat Tati Dior - "Drip": Hip-Hop & Pop Done Right

Indiefferential Published on July 11, 2024

Cloud Ray is a multi-faceted Jamaican-American artist and producer who seamlessly blends hip-hop, pop, and EDM into a vibrant sonic tapestry. Hailing from New York City, but raised in the diverse cultural landscape of New Jersey, Cloud Ray draws inspiration from a rich musical palette, citing iconic figures like Kanye West, Usher, Ludacris, Pitbull, The Black Eyed Peas, Nicki Minaj, and J. Cole as key influences. His ultimate ambition is to harness the power of music to forge connections, unify communities, and create moments of shared joy and understanding.


Having already made a significant impact on the music scene, Cloud Ray's debut EP, "Urban City Elegance," produced in collaboration with acclaimed DJ Treadway, has amassed over one million streams globally, resonating with listeners worldwide. His dynamic performances have graced the stages of renowned venues such as SOBs in New York City and The Franklin Theatre in Tennessee, further solidifying his reputation as an electrifying live act. Notably, Cloud Ray's talent and expertise were showcased on VH1's Love and Hip Hop: New York, where he served as the engineer for hip-hop power couple Remy Ma and Papoose.

From the very outset, "Drip" is an inescapable, flawless blend of nostalgic and modern pieces, including sound production. Currently embarking on an extensive tour spanning from March to November 2024, Cloud Ray is making his mark on the international stage, captivating audiences in Europe with his infectious energy and genre-defying sound. This ambitious endeavor marks a significant milestone in his career, as he expands his reach and shares his musical vision with a global audience.
