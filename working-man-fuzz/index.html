Working Man - Fuzz

By George S.
March 18, 2022

It’s #fuzzfriday! Today we have the killer @dandrivepedal Working Man fuzz, @owenbarrymusic ‘s signature pedal. Easy to dial in, this one sports two kinds of transistors you can choose from for even more tonal options. Love the relic red colour on this one!🔥 Special thanks to @southernguitars for outstanding service to get this one here to Canada. Brian is an ace of a human!🙂

Owen Barry’s signature fuzz, a beautiful sounding silicon fuzz face variant with a BC109C transistor in the first stage and a switchable BC109C/BC183C transistor in the second stage. This pedal has unreal clean up, I’ve never heard a silicon fuzz face clean up as well. Pedal is working perfectly and has a nice relic finish that was done by Dan.
